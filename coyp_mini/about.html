About Healthy Buddy

Our Mission

Healthy Buddy is your AI-powered health assistant, designed to monitor your well-being, predict health risks, and provide personalized recommendations for fitness, diet, and mental health. Our goal is to empower users to lead healthier, balanced lives with smart insights.

Our Features

  • Health Risk Assessment: Monitor BP, sugar levels, lifestyle risks.
  • AI Prediction: Predict potential health issues based on symptoms (coming soon).
  • Mental Health Tracking: Stress, mood, and anxiety evaluation with suggestions.
  • AI Diet & Fitness Planner: Personalized calorie and macro recommendations.
  • Dashboard: Visualize your health data, trends, and insights.
  • Premium Awareness: Games, videos, and daily routines to maintain well-being.

Terms & Conditions

  • Healthy Buddy provides guidance and recommendations, not medical diagnosis.
  • Users should consult certified medical professionals for serious health concerns.
  • All recommendations are based on the data entered by the user.
  • We do not guarantee any specific health outcomes.

Privacy & Data Security

User data is stored securely in your browser using localStorage. We do not share personal information with third parties. All data is private and used only to generate reports and personalized insights.

Awareness & Tips

Maintain a healthy lifestyle by balancing physical activity, diet, and mental well-being. Check our Dashboard regularly to track your progress and follow recommended routines and tips.
